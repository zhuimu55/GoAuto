### 进口产品的邮件范例：商业询盘邮件

```plaintext
Dear Sir or Madam,

This is Amy from ABC Trading in China. We mainly import machines for manufacturers here.

I visited your website and am interested in importing your injection machines. Could you please provide me with an overview of your products? 

Additionally, I would like to know:
1. Have you exported items to China before?
2. Do you have any testing reports available?
3. What is your quality guarantee policy?

I look forward to hearing from you soon!

Thanks & best regards,  
Amy  
(Purchasing Manager)
```

```
尊敬的先生或女士：

我是来自中国ABC贸易公司的Amy。我们主要为这里的制造商进口机械设备。

我访问了贵公司的网站，并对进口贵公司的注塑机感兴趣。能否请您提供一份产品概述？

此外，我想了解以下信息：
1. 贵公司之前是否有向中国出口过产品？
2. 是否有相关的测试报告可供参考？
3. 贵公司的质量保证政策是什么？

期待您的回复！

此致  
敬礼  
Amy  
（采购经理）
```
